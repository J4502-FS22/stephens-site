Coffee spots you need to try in Iowa City

Finding yourself in eastern Iowa and in need of coffee? Here are local spots you can't go wrong with (in no particular order) from a local herself.

Press Coffee

1120 N. Dodge St.
Iowa City, IA 52245
(319) 887-1500
Find them online at Presscoffeeco.com

Press Coffee, which recently relocated to Iowa City from Coralville, is a perfect place to go get things done or just cozy up with a book and a drink. With various study spot options inside, games and some of the best chai on the market, it's a great place to hang out or grab coffee on-the-go.

Java House

211 1/2 E Washington St.
Iowa City, IA 52240
(319) 341-0012
Find them online at thejavahouse.com

Java House is an Iowa City staple, with several locations throughout town. If you want to blend in with the locals or grab a drink on Iowa's campus, Java House is your best bet. Their espresso drinks are best, and if you're hungry, stop by their sister restaraunt Heirloom for sandwiches and salads.

Bluebird Diner

650 W Cherry St #9
North Liberty, IA 52317
(319) 626-2603
Find them online at bluebird.cafe

Bluebird is one of the best diner's in town, with locations in downtown Iowa City and North Liberty. With their recently-added espresso bar, you can get their classic drip coffee or mix it up with a dirty chai, latte or cappuccino. If you go, don't skip out on their breakfast or lunch foods, which are some of the best in town.

Trumpet Blossom

310 E Prentiss St.
Iowa City, IA 52240
(319) 248-0077
Find them online at trumpetblossom.com

Trumpet Blossom is a vegetarian and vegan-friendly spot hidden in the outskirts of downtown. Their drink menu features specialty drinks with flavors like cinnamon, rose or turmeric, and coffee ice cubes to top it off. If you go, consider getting their sweet potato fries to fill up on.

High Ground Cafe

301 E Market St.
Iowa City, IA 52245
(319) 338-5382
Find them online at highgroundcafe.com

High Ground's downtown location is a great place to go for a pick me up when getting work done. Their iced chais and lattes are hard to beat, and the large windows and active atmosphere is perfect for working on school work, personal projects or chatting with friends.

Prairie Lights

15 S Dubuque St.
Iowa City, IA 52240
(319) 337-2681
Find them online at prairielightsbooks.com

Prairie Lights is more of an experience than a coffee shop, but you can't leave without a treat from the cafe upstairs. Prairie Lights is an independent bookstore that frequently supports local authors, so shop local and grab a book and a treat to wrap up with for winter.

Encounter Cafe

376 S Clinton St.
Iowa City, IA 52240
(319) 519-2044
Find them online at the-encounter-cafe.square.site

Encounter Cafe is a newer spot in town that not only has good coffee, but good snacks, too. They focus on gluten, vegan and dairy-free friendly customers, and their breakfast sandwiches and smoothies are unbeatable.
